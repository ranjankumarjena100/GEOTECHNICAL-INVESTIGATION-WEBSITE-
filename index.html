About Our Company

Bureau Veritas Geotechnical Investigation provides professional soil testing and geotechnical engineering services for residential buildings, infrastructure, highways and industrial projects. Our services help engineers and builders determine safe foundation design and soil bearing capacity using modern field and laboratory testing equipment.
